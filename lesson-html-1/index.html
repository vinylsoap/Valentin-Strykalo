Valentin Strykalo

"Valentin Strykalo" is a Ukrainian rock band founded in 2010 by lead singer Yuriy Kaplan

Kaplan founded the band under the influence of rock bands such as Spleen and Radiohead. In 2012, the band released their debut album, Smiris i rasslabisya!, which was mostly written in the comedy rock genre, although it did contain songs with a serious character.

  2. Stanislav Murashko
